Touch ID
A password
You will never forget.
The advanced security and convenience of Touch ID help unlock your Idea Box with just the touch of a finger. Fingerprint can be used to access locked documents, notes, and system settings, without entering your password. With Touch ID, your private information stays secure.

Security Chip
Idea Box features the Idea10 Security Chip making Idea Box even more secure. So when you use Touch ID to unlock your Idea Box, authenticate a document, or pay online, your information stays safe. With on-the-fly data encryption, all your data stored on the SSD is automatically and fully encrypted.
